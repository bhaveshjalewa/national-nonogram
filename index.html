How To Play

  • Fill white cells using digits 1–9.
  • Numbers must add up to the clue value.
  • No duplicate digits allowed in a sum group.
  • Each diagonal clue cell may contain a Down clue, Right clue, or both.

Controls

  • Click a white cell → Use number pad to enter digit.
  • Click again → Change number.
  • Use Undo to revert last move.

Competition Rules

  • Enter your name before starting.
  • Timer starts when game begins.
  • Only one correct submission allowed per device.
  • Do not refresh the page.
