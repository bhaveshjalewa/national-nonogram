How to Play

Each number represents a group of consecutive filled (black) cells. Numbers on the left apply to rows. Numbers on the top apply to columns. If multiple numbers appear, they represent separate groups. There must be at least one empty cell between groups.

Controls

Left Click → Fill a cell (Black)
Right Click → Mark a cell with X

Competition Rules

• Enter your name before starting.
• Timer begins when the puzzle starts.
• Only one submission attempt is allowed.
• Do not refresh the page during competition.
• A submission code will appear after correct completion.
