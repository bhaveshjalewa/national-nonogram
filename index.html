How To Play

  • Fill white cells with numbers 1–9.
  • Numbers in each group must add up to the clue.
  • No digit can repeat inside the same group.
  • Each clue cell may contain horizontal, vertical, or both clues.

Controls

  • Select a white cell.
  • Use number pad to enter digit.
  • Use Undo to revert last move.

Competition Rules

  • Timer starts when game begins.
  • Once correct submission is made, board locks.
  • Do not refresh during competition.

Example (Solved Kakuro)

7 9 8
