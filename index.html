How To Play
  • Each number represents a group of consecutive filled (black) cells.
  • Multiple numbers mean separate groups.
  • There must be at least one empty cell between groups.
  • Numbers on left apply to rows. Numbers on top apply to columns.
Controls
  • Left Click → Fill cell (Black)
  • Right Click → Mark cell with X (Blank)
Competition Rules
  • Enter your name before starting.
  • Timer starts when game begins.
  • Only one submission allowed.
  • Do not refresh the page during competition.
