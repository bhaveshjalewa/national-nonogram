How To Play

Each number represents a group of consecutive filled (black) cells. Multiple numbers mean separate groups with at least one empty cell between them.

Controls

Left Click → Fill cell (Black)
Right Click → Mark cell with X (Blank)

Competition Rules

• Enter your name before starting.
• Timer starts when game begins.
• Only one submission allowed.
• Do not refresh the page during the competition.
