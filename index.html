How To Play

  • Each number represents consecutive filled (black) cells.
  • Multiple numbers mean separate groups.
  • There must be at least ONE blank cell between groups.
  • Left numbers → Row clues. Top numbers → Column clues.

Controls

  • Left Click → Fill (Black)
  • Right Click → X Mark

Competition Rules

  • Enter your name before starting.
  • Timer starts when game begins.
  • Only one submission allowed.
  • Do not refresh page.

Example (Solved 5×5 Nonogram)

Notice how clue 1 1 1 requires blank cells between groups. Also, filled cells satisfy both row and column clues simultaneously.
